Course webpage for the NYU Spring 2023 Course Special Topics in Data Science, DS-GA 3001-009 (Introduction to Computer Vision). This course aims to cover a broad topics in computer vision, and is *not* primarily a deep learning course. We will covert topics in traditional computer vision such as camera geometry, image formation, segmentation, object recognition, classification, and detection (see [Syllabus](#Syllabus)).

<a name="Syllabus"></a>
### Syllabus 
  * Introduction
  * Camera geometry and calibration
  * Filtering and feature detection
  * Radiometry and color
  * Texture and image segmentation
  * Stereopsis
  * Structure from motion and 3D models from images
  * Object recognition - historical perspective
  * CNNs for object classification and detection
  * 3D CNNs, Applications in Medical Imaging
  * Weakly-supervised and unsupervised approaches to image and video interpretation
